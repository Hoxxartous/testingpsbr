Help & Information
Timezone Changes

When you change the timezone:

  • New orders will use the new timezone
  • Existing orders will be displayed in the new timezone
  • All users will see times in the new timezone
  • Reports and analytics will use the new timezone
Best Practices
  • Set timezone to match your restaurant's location
  • Avoid changing timezone frequently
  • Inform staff about timezone changes
  • Test the timezone before peak hours
Qatar Timezone

Asia/Qatar is the recommended timezone for Qatar-based restaurants. It follows Arabia Standard Time (AST) which is UTC+3.
